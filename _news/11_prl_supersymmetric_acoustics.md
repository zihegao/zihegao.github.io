Our [PRL paper](https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/7dqk-h2pg), "Supersymmetric Isophase Acoustic Potentials", is now online. We demonstrate supersymmetric transformations in acoustics through reconfigurable acoustic metamaterials that preserve broadband scattering characteristics.
